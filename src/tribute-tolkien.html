July 15, 1915
Commissioned as temporary second lieutenant in the Lancashire Fusiliers.

MARRIAGE

Married his lifelong partner, Edith in 1916 while an enlisted man in the Great War.

DEATH

September 2, 1973 (aged 81)
Bournermouth, England
